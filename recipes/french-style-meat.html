Description

layered, oven-baked meat, onions, potatoes, and mushrooms, topped with a rich, melted cheese-mayonnaise mixture.

Ingredients

  • 1 pork steak
  • 1 onion
  • 1 large potato
  • 60g strong cheddar cheese
  • 80ml sour cream
  • salt an pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/160°C Fan.
  2. Line a baking sheet with baking parchment.
  3. Separate half the onion slices into rings and lay them on the parchment (above left).
  4. Lay the slices of potato on top of the onion and season with salt and pepper (above right). You can add any extra slices of potato to the pan.
  5. Lay the meat on top of the potatoes and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Arrange the rest of the onion on top of the meat (above right).
  7. Sprinkle the grated cheese over the onions (above left).
  8. Top with your choice of whatever creamy ingredient you prefer (above right).
  9. Finish with a final dusting of salt and pepper.
  10. Bake for 50 minutes, turning the tray around after 25 minutes to ensure even cooking.
  11. Serve hot, with steamed green vegetables.
