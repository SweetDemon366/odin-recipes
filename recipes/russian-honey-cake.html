Russian honey cake

Home

Description

Russian Honey Cake, is a beloved, multi-layered dessert featuring thin, caramelized, cookie-like honey cakes sandwiched between a tangy, light sour cream or sweetened condensed milk frosting.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup wildflower honey
  • 3 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 14 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into slices
  • ¾ cup wildflower honey
  • 2 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¾ teaspoon fine salt
  • 6 large cold eggs
  • 3 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups cold heavy whipping cream
  • ¾ cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Pour 3/4 cup wildflower honey into a deep saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Place a large metal bowl over the lowest heat setting on the stovetop.
  4. Meanwhile, combine baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a small bowl.
  5. Whisk butter mixture and let sit until very warm to the touch.
  6. Sift in flour in 2 or 3 additions, stirring well after each, until batter is easily spreadable.
  7. Transfer about 1/2 cup batter onto the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, 6 to 7 minutes.
  9. Repeat Steps 6 and 7 until you have 8 cake layers, letting each cool on an individual parchment round.
  10. Trim edges using a pizza wheel to ensure they are the same size.
  11. Return scraps to the oven and bake until browned, 7 to 10 minutes more. Let cool completely, 15 to 20 minutes.
  12. Transfer to a resealable bag and beat into fairly fine crumbs using a rolling pin.
  13. Remove the bowl and whisk from the refrigerator.
  14. Place a cake layer on a parchment paper round on a pizza pan or serving plate.
  15. Frost top and sides of cake.
  16. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 8 hours to overnight.
